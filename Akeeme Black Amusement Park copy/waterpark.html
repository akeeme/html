Arcadium's water rides constitute a park within the park.


You can experience the thrill of the Big Dipper, Arcadium's 10-story-high water slide. Or enjoy the twisting, curving ride of Arcadium's underground water ride, the Black Hole. For a ride gentler on the stomach, take a few laps on the Lazy River in a one-, two-, or four-person inner tube. No day is complete without a visit of one of our two wavepools.
