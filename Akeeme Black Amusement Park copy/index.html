The park is located 5 miles northwest of Derby - close to many of Georgia's scenic wonders. Arcadium supports over 70 rides, including some of the state's most exciting roller coasters and water rides. There's also plenty of fun for the younger kids. The park provides two separate kiddie pools and special rides for the kids.

  • April 1 to Memorial Day weekend: 10am to 5pm
  • Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day weekend: 9am to 11pm
  • Labor Day weekend to October 31: 10am to 5pm
