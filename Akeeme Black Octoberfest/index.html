Welcome To Our First Annual

Octoberfest!

Hello all, my team at Akeeme Gardens have decided to host our first annual Octoberfest. We have a country farm located at 123 Farm Road and we would like to invite children to our Octoberfest which starts at the begininng of the fall season. (Adults are also invited)

Apples!

We have a variety of apples for your kids to choose from. Some types of apples you can choose from include; Honeycrisp, Gala, Golden, Empire, Fuji, and even candy apples. All our apples are juicy and delicious so make sure to come and grab some!

Pumpkins!

Come in and grab yourself a pumpkin to carve for holloween or just to take home and eat! We have Jack-O-Lantern's that you can come and fully customize yourself or grab a pre-made custom. You can also grab yourself a regular pumpkin and we will provide the tools to customize yourself.

Grapes!

We also have grapes at our farm, come and pick some grapes to eat. We have black grapes, red grapes, and green grapes. We have from seedless to grapes with seeds.

Halloween Goods!

Come to our farm and pick up all the goods you need for Holloween! We have pumpkin baskets to put your holloween candy in and many other types and shpaes of baskets that will match your liking. We also have candy on deck for our customers to give you a little early taste of holloween! As stated before we also have Jack-O-Lanterns and other types of holloween decorations. So what are you waiting for? Come and stop by now!
